<h2>STGraph - wrap</h2>
<u>Format</u>: <code>wrap(x,y)</code>
<u>Constraints</u>: <code>x</code> and <code>y</code> are arrays (the constraints are specified <a href="fun/_diadic_en.txt">here</a>)
<u>Description</u>: <code>x</code> modulus <code>y</code> also dealing with negative values

While <code>mod(-2,5)==-2</code>, <code>wrap(-2,5)==3</code>.
<br><code>wrap</code> is <a href="functions_en.properties">a mathematical function</a><br><code>wrap</code> is <a href="fun/_diadic_en.txt">a diadic polymorphic function</a>

<u>Exceptions</u>: As specified <a href="fun/_diadic_en.txt">here</a>
