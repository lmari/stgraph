<h2>STGraph - integral</h2>
<u>Format</u>: <code>integral(x)</code>
<u>Constraints</u>: <code>x</code> is an array
<u>Description</u>: in state transitions of state nodes, iterative sum of <code>x</code>, according to the chosen integration algorithm

In the case of the Euler algorithm, the value is <code>this+x*timeD</code>.
<br><code>integral</code> is <a href="functions_en.properties">a mathematical function</a>

<u>Exceptions</u>: no
