<h2>STGraph - sign</h2>
<u>Format</u>: <code>sign(x)</code>
<u>Constraints</u>: <code>x</code> is an array
<u>Description</u>: sign of <code>x</code>, i.e., <code>1</code> if <code>x > 0</code>, <code>-1</code> if <code>x < 0</code>, <code>0</code> if <code>x == 0</code>
