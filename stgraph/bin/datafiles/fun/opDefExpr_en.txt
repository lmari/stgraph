<h2>STGraph - DefExpr</h2>
<u>Format</u>: <code>{x}</code>
<u>Constraints</u>: <code>x</code> is an expression
<u>Description</u>: subexpression that is assigned the variable <code>$wn</code>, where <code>n</code> ranges from 0 to 3 according to the order of evaluation
<br><code>DefExpr</code> is <a href="functions_en.properties">an operator</a>

<u>Exceptions</u>: no
