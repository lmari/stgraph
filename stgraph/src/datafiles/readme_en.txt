<h2>STGraph - Readme</h2>
STGraph is an application for creating, editing, and executing models of dynamical systems according to the state-variable approach to Systems Theory.
It has been firstly developed to support lab and home works of undergraduate and graduate students of Engineering courses, but it has been used also by high school and master students.
STGraph is the preferred editor to create models accessible over the web through STGraphWeb.
