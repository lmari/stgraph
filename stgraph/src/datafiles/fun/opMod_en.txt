<h2>STGraph - Mod</h2>
<u>Format</u>: <code>x%y</code>
<u>Constraints</u>: <code>x</code> and <code>y</code> are arrays (the constraints are specified <a href="fun/_diadic_en.txt">here</a>)
<u>Description</u>: <code>x</code> modulus <code>y</code>

Equivalent to the function <code>mod(x,y)</code>.
<br><code>Mod</code> is <a href="functions_en.properties">an operator</a><br><code>Mod</code> is <a href="fun/_diadic_en.txt">a diadic polymorphic function</a>

<u>Exceptions</u>: As specified <a href="fun/_diadic_en.txt">here</a>.
