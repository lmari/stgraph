<h2>STGraph - exponential</h2>
<u>Format</u>: <code>exponential(v,x,s)</code>
<u>Constraints</u>: <code>v</code> (optional) is a vector; <code>x</code> (optional) is an array; <code>s</code> (optional) is a scalar, either <code>0</code> or <code>1</code>
<u>Description</u>: exponential probability distribution of parameters <code>v=[p1]</code>, where <code>p1</code>=lambda (<code>p1>0</code>; default value: <code>1</code>): random number extracted from the distribution if <code>x</code> and <code>s</code> are not specified; value of the pdf, if <code>s==0</code> or the cdf, if <code>s==1</code>, at the point <code>x</code>
<br><code>exponential</code> is <a href="functions_en.properties">a statistical function</a><br><code>exponential</code> is <a href="fun/_distrib_en.txt">a statistical distribution function</a>

<u>Exceptions</u>: All elements of <code>x</code> must be strictly positive; <code>s</code> must be either <code>0</code> or <code>1</code>
