<h2>STGraph - bline</h2>
<u>Format</u>: <code>bline(vx,vy,x)</code>
<u>Constraints</u>: <code>vx</code> and <code>vy</code> are vectors; <code>x</code> is an array
<u>Description</u>: the <code>y</code> value corresponding to <code>x</code> over the segment from <code>(vx[0],vy[0])</code> to <code>(vx[1],vy[1])</code>, and constant elsewhere
<br><code>bline</code> is <a href="functions_en.properties">a mathematical function</a><br><code>bline</code> is <a href="fun/_interp_en.txt">an interpolation function</a>

<u>Exceptions</u>: Both <code>vx</code> and <code>vy</code> must have two elements; <code>vx[0]</code> must be less than <code>vx[1]</code>.
